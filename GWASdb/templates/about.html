About GWAS Manager


The Baxter Lab GWAS Manager was created to manage the many Genome Wide Association Studies conducted in the lab. The GWAS Manager serves as a central repository for all the the data associated with a GWAS, including the results.


Users can view past GWAS experiments. Search functionality is under development with the eventual goal of allowing users to search through GWAS experiments on a number of different criteria.


Users can also use this site to add a new GWAS experiment to GWAS Manager.
